# Questionnaires

This repository contains the questionnaires used as a basis
for collecting information in the project.

The questionnaires were used as a general framework.
However, they were not followed rigidly in every situation.

Instead, the questions and level of detail were adapted
depending on the specific context, the type of facility,
and the information available during each interaction.

The purpose of the questionnaires was to support a structured
collection of relevant data, while still allowing flexibility
for case-specific conditions.
